Brief overview of best compositions of patch 13.15. When & how to play them

Jerry and friends


Team Comp Description:

This comp uses a full Gunner backline to melt down enemy teams in a matter of seconds!

When to make:

– If you get Piltover trait active at Stage 2-1 or 2-2

Deadeye Aphelios


Team Comp Description:

This comp uses a strong Deadeye Backline with beefy Brawler like Sejuani as your main tank in the front!

When to make:

– If you get a Deadeye Augment
– If you see an early copy of Sion

Ionia Yasuo


Team Comp Description:

This comp uses a full Ionia team with Yasuo as your main carry and Shen as your main tank!

When to make:

– If you get an Ionia Augment

Full power Sorcerers


Team Comp Description:

This comp uses a full Sorcerer team and wants you to turn Jarvan into a Sorcerer with Lux in the back as your main carry!

When to make:

– If you get a Sorcerer Augment

Noxus Darius & Katarina


Team Comp Description:

This comp uses a full Noxus team with the two 3-costs Darius and Katarina as your main carries!

When to make:

– If you get a Noxus Augment
– If you see an early copy of Sion
– If you’re the only one in your lobby to play Darius / Katarina

Bite everything !


Team Comp Description:

This comp uses a full Bruiser team with 3 star Rek’Sai and Kai’Sa as your main Void carries!

When to make:

– If you get a Bruiser Augment
– If you see lots of Rek’Sai/Lissandra copies to 3 star them

Azir Lux Ascend


Team Comp Description:

This comp sets the focus on Strategists with Azir as your main Damage carry, Nasus as your hybrid tank, and Jarvan and K’Sante as your leftover utility units!

When to make:

– When you get a Strategist related Augment
– When you get a Shurima Emblem/Augment
– When you’re the only player in your lobby to play Strategist
